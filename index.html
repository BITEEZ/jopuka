Insurance

Jopuka performs all work at "OWNERS RISK" under Limited liability risk. The owner of the cargo is expected to take out appropriate insurance covers including Warehousing Risks and Goods in Transit Insurance covering all risks or assume the risk of loss if such insurance is not taken out.

How long does it take to clear my shipments?

Shipments take between 1-2 days to clear. Please note documentations should be received 4 days prior to shipment arrival.

What are the most common reasons for delay of my goods at the port?

This includes and not limited to; Failure to forward clearance documents before the flight/vessel arrives or Advance Payment not done in good time, various challenges with KPA

What documentation do I need to provide for Air or Sea shipments?

Documents Include:Commercial Invoice/Proforma Invoice, Packing List, Certificate of Conformity(COC), AWB/BL ,Insurance Cover Note, PRO 1B forms from the Ministry of foreign affairs if Diplomatic cargo
